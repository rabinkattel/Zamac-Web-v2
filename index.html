About Us

A community passionate about diecast car model craftsmanship

Zamac Nepal is the premier community for diecast car model collectors in Nepal, creating a vibrant space for enthusiasts to connect and share their love for precision-crafted models.

We host exclusive events, exhibitions, and workshops to showcase the artistry of diecast car models and inspire creativity among collectors.

Join our growing community and immerse yourself in the world of diecast car collecting in Nepal.
