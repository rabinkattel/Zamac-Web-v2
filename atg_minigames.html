About Us

A community passionate about scale model craftsmanship

The Nepalese Scale Models Collectors Community is a dedicated group of scale model enthusiasts who come together to share their passion for collecting and showcasing scale models.

We organize events, exhibitions, and meet-ups for collectors to connect, learn, and enjoy their hobby.

Join us in celebrating the artistry and craftsmanship of scale models, and become part of a growing community!
