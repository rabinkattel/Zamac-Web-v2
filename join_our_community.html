Why Join Us?

The Nepalese Scale Models Collectors Community, Zamac Nepal, is the heart of diecast car model enthusiasm in Nepal. By joining us, you’ll connect with a vibrant group of collectors who share your passion for precision-crafted scale models. Gain access to exclusive events like our annual diecast exhibitions, hands-on customization workshops, and collector meet-ups where you can showcase your collection and exchange ideas with like-minded enthusiasts.

As a member, you’ll enjoy unique opportunities to expand your knowledge and skills. Participate in trading sessions to acquire rare models, attend expert-led talks on the history and craftsmanship of diecast cars, and learn advanced techniques for model restoration and display. Our community fosters a welcoming environment for both seasoned collectors and newcomers, ensuring everyone has a chance to grow and connect.

Beyond events, Zamac Nepal offers access to exclusive resources, including early notifications about upcoming releases, curated guides on sourcing authentic models, and a private online network for sharing tips and stories. Your collection deserves a community that celebrates its value—join us to be recognized and inspired among Nepal’s top diecast enthusiasts.

Ready to dive into the world of diecast collecting? Follow us on Instagram to join our community, stay updated on events, and start connecting with collectors across Nepal today!
